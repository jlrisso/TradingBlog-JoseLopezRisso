La Historia

This blog started when I was living in Chicago, then while I was commuting to Chicago and living in the suburbs, now that I live AND work in the suburbs it took me a little bit to find my footing on what to blog about. I still love blogging about new and fun things in Chicago since I’m there so often and love sharing about my travels as well. I want to share more about our home and Lake County in the near future- we will see where this takes me!

This question was probably the most important question I asked myself when I was questioning my blog. I blog to hold myself accountable and hopefully to inspire others to live a balanced, healthy life with a positive attitude. I’ve had people tell me I’ve inspired them before- whether it be making one of my recipes or to get to the gym. That makes it worth it to me!
